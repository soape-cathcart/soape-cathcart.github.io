About Silken Strands

This is the debut for Silken Strands, a collection of natural fiber blouses for Fall 2017.

The line was designed for women desiring classic woven blouses that are flattering, feminine and fun. You will find lighter weight fabrics and shorter sleeves in fall hues and tones in the collection. We want you wearing fall colors while feeling comfortable at an outdoor event or meeting a client for lunch on a warm autumn day. We have added elements such as raglan sleeves, open shoulders, concealed plackets, v-necklines and v-backs, and asymmetry to flatter you. The designs are simple with fabrics which have soft hands in beautiful rich colors. The collection offers blouses in Cotton Swiss Dot, Cotton Chambray, Cotton Voile, Cotton Jacquard, Silk Crepe de Chine and Silk Chiffon.

The line was imagined, considered and dreamed about for some time now. I feel clothes can make a difference in giving us confidence and making us feel our best. Late in 2015, my family and I lost my two older sisters within days of each other. Working on creating the blouse line has helped me with my heartache and grief. The idea of developing beautiful blouses for women has helped me transcend to a happier place. I hope to brighten your day or lifestyle while wearing a blouse from Silken Strands!
